About This Project

This project was and is one of my bootcamp's major Capstone projects which uses the pokeapi. Massive shoutout to those group of people who made a free source for pokemon fans to get large amounts of game data in a few simple lines of code! The main purpose of this project is to have a place where fans of the pokemon games can come and discuss their findings along with sharing tips with other users in this site. I know it's pretty bare at the moment but trust me I have a few things coming down the pipeline that I have listed above in the patch notes section so feel free to poke around and let me know if there are any other features I should be working on including or that you would like to see. Remember, I am limited to the information from the PokeAPI BUT that doesn't mean I can't find ways around this! Feedback on the overall layout is always welcome as well but above all else, I hope you enjoy using this site as much as I enjoyed making it.
-DQ

This Project mainly works with Flask and Flask-SQLAlchemy however, it also includes a bit of Bootstrap, and Javascript for styling and DOM manipulation. The backend database is being run with SQL and PostgreSQL along with Python!
